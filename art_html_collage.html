This is my newest learned medium of art. I make these collages in their own HTML page with inline positioning. There's no CSS or JavaScript. I harvest the .gifs from old web archives like Gifcities. I really think these look so cool and have a lot of potential. I have been spending the past few months perfecting these, and I am currently working on one that (I think) is the best art piece I have ever created. I hope to have it finished sometime soon.

Eventually, you'll be able to see the actual .HTML page and accompanying code for each collage. For now, I have just made .gifs out of the web pages. I'd love to hear feedback on these, as I am extremely proud of them! Click any of the collage titles to view the collage at full resolution in a new tab.
